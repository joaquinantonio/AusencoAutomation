# Company HR Policy (Excerpt)
## Annual Leave Entitlement
Employees are entitled to 18 days of paid annual leave per year after probation. New employees accrue leave monthly.
Unused leave may be carried forward up to 6 days into the next calendar year.
## Sick Leave
Employees are entitled to sick leave as per statutory requirements. A medical certificate is required for absences exceeding two consecutive days.
## Claims & Reimbursement
Employees must submit expense claims within 30 days of the expense date. Receipts above RM50 must be attached.
Approved claims will be reimbursed in the next payroll cycle.
## Overtime
Overtime must be pre-approved by the line manager. Overtime rates follow statutory guidelines.
